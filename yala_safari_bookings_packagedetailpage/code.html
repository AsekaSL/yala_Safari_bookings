Experience the Golden Hour

Our Private All-Inclusive Morning Safari is designed for wildlife enthusiasts who want the best chance of spotting Yala’s elusive leopards. Entering the park as the sun rises, you’ll witness the jungle waking up—a prime time for predator activity. With a private jeep and expert naturalist guide, you can explore at your own pace, lingering at sightings and avoiding the biggest crowds.

Safari Timeline

05:00 AM

Hotel Pickup

We pick you up from your hotel in Tissamaharama or Yala in our rugged safari jeep.

06:00 AM

Park Entry

Gates open. We enter early to be the first on the tracks for fresh leopard prints.

06:30 AM

Golden Hour Tracking

The best light for photography. We search for leopards, sloth bears, and active birdlife.

09:00 AM

Jungle Breakfast

Exit & Drop-off

Return to your hotel just as the mid-day heat sets in.
